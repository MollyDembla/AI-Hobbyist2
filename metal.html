Metal Wall Art

Material Needed:

  • Scrap metal pieces (e.g., old tools, metal sheets)
  • Sandpaper or wire brush
  • Metal primer
  • Paint (optional)
  • Welding equipment or strong adhesive
  • Hanging hardware

Instructions:

  • Prepare the Metal: Clean the metal pieces thoroughly using sandpaper or a wire brush to remove rust and dirt.
  • Design Your Art: Arrange the metal pieces into your desired pattern or shape.
  • Assemble: Weld the pieces together or use a strong adhesive suitable for metal.
  • Finish: Apply a metal primer to prevent rust, then paint if desired.
  • Hang: Attach appropriate hardware to display your art.

Metal Food Tin Storage Containers

Material Needed:

  • Empty metal food tins
  • Soap and water
  • Paint (base and top coats)
  • Painter's tape
  • Paintbrushes
  • Clear sealant (optional)

Instructions:

  • Clean the Tins: Wash the tins with soap and water; let them dry completely.
  • Apply Base Coat: Paint the entire exterior with a base coat; allow it to dry.
  • Masking: Use painter's tape to create patterns or designs
  • Apply Top Coat: Paint over the entire tin with your chosen top coat color.
  • Reveal Design: Once dry, carefully remove the tape to reveal the pattern.
  • Seal (Optional): Apply a clear sealant for durability.

Metal Beads from Aluminum Cans

Material Needed:

  • Empty aluminum cans
  • Scissors
  • Metal file
  • Doming block and punch
  • Hole punch
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint or nail polish (optional)

Instructions:

  • Prepare the Can: Cut the top and bottom off the can; flatten the remaining piece.
  • Cut Shapes: Cut out circles or desired shapes.
  • Smooth Edges: Use a metal file to smooth edges.
  • Form Beads: Place the cut shapes into the doming block; use the punch to create a dome.
  • Create Holes: Punch holes for threading.
  • Decorate: Paint or decorate as desired.

Metal Rolling Cart

Material Needed:

  • Metal rolling cart
  • Steel wool
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint
  • Painter's tape
  • Brushes

Instructions:

  • Remove Rust: Use steel wool to scrub away rust spots.
  • Sand: Sand the entire cart to create a smooth surface.
  • Clean: Wipe down to remove dust.
  • Tape: Use painter's tape to protect areas not being painted.
  • Paint: Apply paint evenly; let dry.
  • Finish: Remove tape and reassemble if necessary.
