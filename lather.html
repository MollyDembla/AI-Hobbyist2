Leather Wallet

Material Needed:

  • Old leather jacket
  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread or sewing machine
  • Wallet pattern
  • Ruler
  • Pen or chalk for making

Instructions:

1. Disassemble the Jacket:

  • Carefully cut along the seams of the jacket to separate usable leather panels.

2.Prepare the Pattern:

  • Print and cut out a wallet pattern of your choice.

3. Trace and Cut Leather:

  • Place the pattern pieces on the leather, trace them using a pen or chalk, and cut them out.

4.Assemble the Wallet:

  • Follow the pattern instructions to sew the pieces together, forming pockets and compartments as needed.

5. Finish Edges:

  • Trim any excess leather and finish the edges for a clean look.

Tote Bag

Material Needed:

  • Large pieces of leather (from an old couch or jacket)
  • Fabric for lining
  • Sewing machine suitable for leather or heavy-duty needle and thread
  • Bag pattern or measurements
  • Scissors
  • Ruler
  • pen or chalk for making

Instructions:

1. Prepare Leather

  • Clean the leather and cut out large, flat sections.

2. Cut Pieces:

  • Based on your bag pattern or desired dimensions, cut out the leather pieces for the front, back, sides, and straps.

3. Optional Lining:

  • Cut fabric pieces for the lining if desired.

4. Assemble Bag:

  • Sew the leather pieces together, attaching the sides to the front and back panels

5. Attach Straps:

  • Sew the straps securely to the bag.

6. Add Lining:

  • If using a lining, sew it to the inside of the bag.

Refurbishing Leather Items

Material Needed:

  • Old leather item (e.g., bag, wallet)
  • Leather cleaner
  • Leather dye
  • Soft cloths
  • Leather conditioner

Instructions:

1.Clean Leather:

  • Use a leather cleaner to remove dirt and oils from the item.

2.Apply Dye:

  • Evenly apply leather dye using a soft cloth, following the product instructions.

3. Dry:

  • Allow the item to dry completely.

3. Condition:

  • Apply leather conditioner to keep the leather supple.
